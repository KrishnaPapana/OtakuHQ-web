Schedule

Full Week Schedule

Mon–Sun tab bar for the current week. Each day loads on demand and caches instantly. Every episode card shows a live countdown — pink/cyan gradient when hours away, gold when less than an hour, orange under 15 minutes, pulsing red when airing now.

♥

Favorites

Save & Track Anime

Heart any anime from the schedule, search results, or releases. Your favorites appear in a dropdown from the ♥ icon in the header, each with a live episode countdown. The toolbar badge shows how many favorites are airing today.

🔔

Alerts

Episode Notifications

Toggle "Episode alerts" to receive a Chrome notification 15 minutes before any favorited episode airs — even with the popup closed. The background service worker checks every minute so you never miss a drop.

🔢

Badge

Toolbar Badge Counter

The extension icon badge updates every minute, showing the number of your favorited shows with an episode airing today. Hover the icon for a precise tooltip.

📈

Hot Now

Trending This Week

A horizontal scroll strip of the top 15 currently trending anime on AniList. Rank badges highlight the top 3; score badges color-code by quality — green, yellow, red.

⌕

Search

Anime Search

Search any title via AniList. Results show cover art, score, format, episode count, and genre tags. Debounced input keeps it fast. Favorite directly from search results.

▶

Watch

Streaming Platforms

Compact icon strips split into Official (Crunchyroll, Netflix, HiDive, Prime Video, Disney+, Muse Asia, and more) and Fan Sites (HiAnime, 9Anime, AnimePahe, Gogoanime, AnimeKai) with live domain resolution. Filter by region or search by name.

📰

Fresh Reads

Anime News

Six recent headlines from Google News, locale-matched to your selected region (All / Global / India / US). One tap opens the source article.

✦

Discover

Top by Genre

Tap any of 16 genre pills — Action, Romance, Psychological, Mecha, Horror, Slice of Life, and more — to browse the highest-scored anime in that genre, with pagination.

Permissions at a glance

PermissionWhy it's needed
storage Save region preference, favorites, notification toggle, domain cache, and sent-alert log — all locally on your device.
alarms Run a 1-minute background check to fire episode alerts and update the badge counter.
notifications Show a Chrome notification 15 minutes before a favorited episode airs.
graphql.anilist.co Fetch schedule, releases, trending, search, and genre data from AniList's public API.
news.google.com Fetch the anime news RSS feed.
<all_urls> Follow HTTP redirects to discover current working domains for fan streaming sites. OtakuHQ does not read or modify any web page you visit.

About OtakuHQ

OtakuHQ does not host or stream anime content. It is an anime discovery and information companion that links to official streaming services and displays publicly available data from AniList and Google News. No user data is uploaded or shared. All preferences and favorites are stored locally in your browser.
